Privacy Disclosure

  • Information collected: OpenAI API key and the text you ask the extension to rephrase or summarize. The extension may also read selected text or page content to perform these actions. Data is stored locally in your browser.
  • Use of information: The API key is used to send requests to OpenAI on your behalf. The text you submit is sent to OpenAI only to generate responses and is not used for other purposes by this extension.
  • Information sharing: Requests (including your text and API key) are shared with OpenAI to provide the service. The extension does not share your data with any other parties.
  • Retention and control: Your encrypted API key is stored locally in browser storage until you remove it. You can remove it at any time from the extension settings.
